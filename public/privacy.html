POLICY.

Your privacy is fundamental to our agency operations. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your digital data.

1. Data Collection

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it via our lead forms, contact requests, or direct emails. This typically includes your name, email address, phone number, and project details.

2. Usage of Information

  • To provide tailored digital marketing strategies and quotes.
  • To communicate regarding project updates or inquiries.
  • To share relevant marketing insights (you may opt out at any time).

3. Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to ensure your personal information is protected from unauthorized access or disclosure. We do not sell or trade your personal data to third parties.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic patterns via standard analytics tools. This data is anonymized and used for structural improvements only.

5. Lead Form Data

Data submitted via our "Free Strategy Call" popup is stored securely and accessed only by our authorized sales and strategy team members to facilitate your consultation.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data stored with us. Contact sales@creashift.in for any privacy-related requests.
