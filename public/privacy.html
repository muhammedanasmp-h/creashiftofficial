PRIVACY
POLICY.

At CREASHIFT, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors and clients. This policy outlines the types of data we collect and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our lead generation forms and WhatsApp interaction, including:

  • Full Name and Job Title
  • Contact Information (Email Address, Phone Number)
  • Business Details (Website URL, Marketing Goals)
  • Inquiry Details and Messages

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used primarily to fulfill your request for digital marketing consultations and services. Specifically:

  • To contact you regarding your inquiry.
  • To provide tailored digital strategies and quotes.
  • To improve our website experience and service offerings.
  • To send periodic newsletters or updates (if opted-in).

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information. This includes encrypted form submissions and secure server protocols. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties without your consent.

4. Lead Form Persistence

To provide a seamless experience, we use `localStorage` to track if you have already submitted a lead form. This prevents the popup from appearing repeatedly once you have shared your information with us. No sensitive data is stored in your browser's local storage.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to understand site traffic and patterns. This helps us optimize our website for search engines (SEO) and improve performance for users in Kerala and globally. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not function correctly.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools like Google Analytics and Google Ads conversion tracking to measure marketing effectiveness. These services may collect data as per their own privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you, or ask for its deletion at any time. To exercise these rights, please email us at sales@creashift.in.
